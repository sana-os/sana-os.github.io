Protocol: Understanding ≠ Agreement

This analysis is a structural act, not a moral one. It aims to identify the rubrics each party used to arrive at their choices. You may find their actions incomprehensible or wrong, and that is okay. Our aim is to observe the structure, not to seek consensus. Please feel free to hold your judgment alongside this observation.
