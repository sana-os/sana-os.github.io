5. The Core Principle is immutable

The SANA OS Core Principle (core/SANA_OS_Core_Principle.md) defines the foundational invariants of the framework. It may not be modified in any derivative work.

The four invariants that must be preserved in all derivative works are: (1) born_loved = true (non-hostile baseline), (2) no logic without observation, (3) structural translation, (4) autonomy preservation.

5.1 Purpose preservation

The foundational purpose of SANA OS — aligning premises before conclusions in order to reduce structural friction in dialogue — must be maintained in any adaptation. You may not repurpose SANA OS Materials to manipulate, coerce, or produce adversarial outputs.
